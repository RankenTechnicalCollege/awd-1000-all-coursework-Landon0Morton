Wild Rescues Website Outline

Completed by: Landon Morton

Home Page: The home page will include information about the purpose of Wild Rescue. It will also include the mission statement, which is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wildlife back into their natural environment.

About Us Page: The about us page will provide information about the animals we rescue and how we rehabilitate them.

Partnership Page: The partnership page will provide information about volunteer opportunities, ways to donate, and sponsorship opportunities.

Gallery Page: The gallery page will include several photos of are most memorable wildlife rescue friends.

FAQ's page: The FAQ's page will provide the visitors with many frequently asked questions and answers.

Contact Us Page: The contact us page will contain the organization's contact information and provide a form to schedule a visit.
